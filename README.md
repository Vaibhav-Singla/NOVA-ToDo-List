# NOVA-ToDo-List
Allows user to plan and manage their tasks

Add a Todo: Go to the Home page, enter a title and description for your todo, and click "Submit".
View Todos: All your todos will be listed on the Home page under "Your Todos".
Update a Todo: Click on the "Update" button next to a todo to modify its title or description.
Delete a Todo: Click on the "Delete" button next to a todo to remove it from the list.
Search: Use the search bar at the top to find specific todos by title or description.
